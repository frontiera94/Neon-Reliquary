name: Jabbing Style — Trigger Conditions
description: Jabbing Style requires unarmed strikes specifically; does not trigger on manufactured monk weapons; per-hit after first, per target
type: reference
---

**Source:** Pathfinder RPG: Melee Tactics Toolbox (or Inner Sea Combat).

**Trigger:** When you hit a target with an unarmed strike AND you have already hit that same target with an unarmed strike earlier in the same round.

**Key restrictions:**
- **Only unarmed strikes** — manufactured weapons (Knuckle Axe, Nunchaku, etc.) do NOT trigger Jabbing Style even during flurry
- Per-target tracking — hitting Target A twice gives +1d6 on second hit; hitting Target B once does not benefit
- **Each hit after the first** gets +1d6 (not just the second hit) — so three unarmed hits on same target: hit 1 normal, hit 2 = +1d6, hit 3 = +1d6
- The extra damage is not multiplied on critical hits

**Upgrade chain:**
- Jabbing Style: +1d6 on 2nd+ hits
- Jabbing Master (requires Jabbing Style + BAB 9): +2d6
- Jabbing Dancer (requires Jabbing Master + BAB 12): +4d6 if moved 5+ feet before attacking

**Agelmund (Monk 4):** Jabbing Style correctly encoded. Knuckle Axe does not benefit. Ki extra attack adds a third unarmed hit opportunity, making Jabbing Style activate twice per flurry if all three hit the same target.
