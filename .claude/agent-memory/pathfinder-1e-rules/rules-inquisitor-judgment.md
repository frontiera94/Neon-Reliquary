**Simultaneous Judgment Count:**
- Levels 1-7: 1 simultaneous judgment
- Levels 8-15: 2 simultaneous judgments (Second Judgment class feature at level 8)
- Levels 16+: 3 simultaneous judgments (Third Judgment at level 16)
- Valerius at level 12: **2 simultaneous judgments**

**Daily Uses:** 1/2 Inquisitor level (round up) — at level 12, 5 uses/day. Switching one active judgment to a new type costs one additional daily use.

**Sacred Bonus Tiers (CRB Inquisitor class table):**
| Inquisitor Level | Bonus Value |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | +1 |
| 4-6 | +2 |
| 7-9 | +3 |
| 10-12 | +4 |
| 13-15 | +5 |
| 16-18 | +6 |
| 19-20 | +7 |

**At level 12, all sacred bonuses are +4.**

**Key Judgment effects at level 12 (+4 sacred tier):**
- Justice: +4 sacred to attack rolls
- Destruction: +4 sacred to weapon damage
- Purity: +4 sacred to all saving throws
- Protection: +4 sacred to AC
- Healing: Fast Healing 4 (4 HP/round)
- Resiliency: DR 4/magic
- Resistance: Energy resistance 16 (one energy type)
- Smiting: Weapon treated as magic + one alignment subtype for DR

**Best offensive loadout:** Justice + Destruction (+4 atk, +4 dmg)

**Bane (level 5+):**
- Swift action activation
- +2 enhancement bonus on attack rolls
- +2d6 bonus damage (vs. designated creature type)
- Lasts rounds/day = Inquisitor level
- Activation: swift action; bane weapon glows

**Greater Bane (level 12 class feature):**
- Attack bonus remains: +2 enhancement (does NOT increase)
- Damage increases: **+4d6** (up from +2d6)
- Everything else identical to Bane
- Valerius: 12 rounds/day of Bane/Greater Bane (already encoded correctly in JSON)
